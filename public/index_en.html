Our Mission and Vision

At SYNVORK, our mission is to extend the lifecycle and efficiency of pharmaceutical manufacturing equipment, thereby contributing to sustainable and cost-effective pharmaceutical production. We envision a future where quality pharmaceutical machinery is accessible to all, promoting innovation and reliability in the industry.

About us

Established in 2023, SYNVORK is a dynamic and forward-thinking company which is Unlocking Excellence in Pharmaceutical Machinery Refurbishment. It is based in France with a vision to reshape the pharmaceutical industry. With registration set for Year 2023, we embark on a mission to redefine excellence in pharmaceutical machinery refurbishing.

Specialized Refurbishing Expertise

Our expertise lies in the refurbishment of a wide range of pharmaceutical machines, including solid dosage form machinery such as blistering, cartoning, tablet press machines, tablet counter machines, and coating machines. We're also proficient in rejuvenating injectable lines, covering vial filling lines, ampoule filling lines, and prefilled syringe lines. In the biopharmaceutical sector, we excel in refurbishing up-stream process equipment like fermenters and rigid bio-reactors, as well as down-stream purification systems.

Reviving Laboratory Equipment

Beyond pharmaceutical machinery, we extend our refurbishing capabilities to laboratory equipment. From ovens to advanced freezers, centrifuges, and chromatography systems, including HPLC, UPLC, GC, and Preparative HPLC, we breathe new life into vital lab instruments.

Our Commitment to Quality

At SYNVORK, quality is our cornerstone. We follow stringent refurbishing processes and quality control measures to ensure that every machine and equipment we refurbish meets or exceeds industry standards. Our team of experts, armed with extensive industry knowledge, is dedicated to delivering reliable, high-performance machinery to our clients.

Sustainability and Cost-Efficiency

Recognizing the significance of sustainability and cost-effectiveness in the contemporary pharmaceutical industry, we embrace the refurbishment of existing machinery as a pivotal step towards waste reduction and a reduced carbon footprint, thereby promoting environmentally conscious pharmaceutical production. Furthermore, our refurbished equipment provides a fiscally responsible alternative to investing in new machinery, empowering businesses to allocate their resources with greater efficiency.
